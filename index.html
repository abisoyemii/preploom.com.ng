Expert Tips to Score 110+ on TOEFL iBT

1

Master Note-Taking for Listening

Develop a shorthand system. Focus on key concepts, transitions, and speaker attitudes. Practice with academic lectures from TED-Ed and university open courses.

2

Reading: Skim Strategically

Spend 2-3 minutes skimming the entire passage before looking at questions. Identify the main idea, structure, and author's purpose first.

3

Speaking: Use Templates

Prepare transition phrases for each task type. Practice timing strictly - 15 seconds preparation, 45-60 seconds response. Record yourself daily.

4

Writing: Plan Before You Type

Spend 2-3 minutes outlining your integrated and independent essays. Use specific examples and clear topic sentences. Aim for 300+ words.

Sample TOEFL iBT Questions

Reading Medium

According to the passage, what is the primary function of the mitochondria in eukaryotic cells?

  • A) To store genetic information for protein synthesis
  • B) To convert nutrients into energy through cellular respiration
  • C) To regulate the cell cycle and division processes
  • D) To transport molecules across the cell membrane
Correct Answer: B
Mitochondria are known as the "powerhouses" of the cell because they generate ATP through oxidative phosphorylation, converting nutrients into usable energy.
Speaking Task 2 Hard

Campus Announcement: The university library will extend its hours until midnight during final exam week. Do you agree or disagree with this policy? Use specific reasons and examples.

Preparation Strategy:
  • State your position clearly in the first sentence
  • Provide 2 specific reasons with examples
  • Use transitions: "First of all," "Furthermore," "In conclusion"
  • Manage your time: 15 seconds prep, 45 seconds response

How long is the TOEFL iBT score valid? +
TOEFL iBT scores are valid for 2 years from the test date. This is standard across all ETS tests. After 2 years, your scores will no longer be accessible in the ETS system, though you can request a score report reprint for tests taken within the last 2 years.
Can I retake the TOEFL iBT if I'm not satisfied with my score? +
Yes, you can retake the test as many times as you wish, but no more than once in a 3-day period. There is no limit to the number of times you can take the test. Your MyBest scores will automatically combine your highest section scores from all valid tests in the last 2 years.
What is the difference between TOEFL iBT and IELTS? +
While both test English proficiency, TOEFL iBT is fully computer-based with American English focus, while IELTS offers paper and computer options with British/Australian English. TOEFL speaking is recorded; IELTS speaking is face-to-face with an examiner. TOEFL is preferred by US institutions; IELTS is more widely accepted in UK, Australia, and Canada.
How soon can I get my TOEFL results? +
Official scores are available approximately 6 days after your test date. You will receive an email notification when your scores are ready. PDF score reports can be downloaded from your ETS account, and official reports can be sent to institutions electronically or by mail.
