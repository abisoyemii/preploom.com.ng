Duolingo English Test Format

Understanding the test structure is key to success. Our course breaks down each section with targeted practice.

🎧

Listening Comprehension

Listen to conversations and lectures, then answer multiple-choice questions. Tests your ability to understand spoken English in academic and everyday contexts.

📖

Reading Comprehension

Read passages and answer questions about main ideas, details, and inferences. Includes academic texts and practical reading materials.

🎤

Conversational Fluency

Respond to prompts by speaking naturally. The AI adapts to your proficiency level, testing pronunciation, vocabulary, and grammar.

✍️

Writing Proficiency

Write responses to prompts testing your ability to express ideas clearly. Includes both short answers and extended writing tasks.

🔄

Adaptive Technology

Questions adjust in difficulty based on your responses. The test becomes easier or harder to accurately measure your English level.

📊

Instant Scoring

Receive your CEFR level and percentile ranking immediately. Detailed feedback helps you understand your strengths and areas for improvement.
