Cookie Policy

This page explains how PrepLoom uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, remember your settings, and support analytics and advertising features.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve our services.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies: needed for basic site functionality, navigation, and security.
  • Preference cookies: remember choices like consent preferences and language selection.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand pages visited, traffic sources, and user behavior in aggregate form.
  • Advertising cookies: used by third-party advertising partners when you consent to personalized advertising.

How we use cookies

We use cookies to keep the website working smoothly, remember your consent choices, measure performance, and offer relevant study resources and advertisements. We do not use cookies to collect unnecessary personal information.

Managing your cookie preferences

You can usually refuse or delete cookies from your browser settings. Please note that blocking some cookies may affect the functionality of parts of our website. When you first visit our site, the consent banner will allow you to choose your preferences.
